Discover the Albatross!

  • Welcome to the Galapagos Islands, home of the majestic albatross!
  • Scientific Name: Diomedea exulans (wandering albatross)
  • Did you know that albatrosses are like the superheroes of the sky? They can fly enormous distances without stopping, a wonder of nature!
  • Conservation Status: They are so special that they are classified as vulnerable, so let's take care of them together!
  • Where to see them? On Espa√±ola Island, they are the stars of the show, especially during the nesting season.

  • Remember to enjoy from a respectful distance so as not to interrupt their daily activities. Have a great trip!
